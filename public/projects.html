World Hunger:
(Nov 2021-Nov 2021)
Associated with borough of Manhattan Community College.

2021 Hackathon organized by BMCC, Three other students and I worked on this project to come up for a solution on solving world Hunger,
I was responsible on implementing HTML, CSS, IBM-Cloud and my counter parts worked on other areas including C++.
Inspiration : Changing the world
What it does: Find Food services
How we built it: C++, Html, CSS, IBM-Cloud
Challenges we ran into: Implementing different languages to approach the issue.
Accomplishments that we're proud of: Putting together a presentation as well as the necessary knowledge and resources that we know in order
to come up with a finish product with a limited amount of time for this competition.
What we learned: Team Building, Time Management, Converting languages to real life products.
